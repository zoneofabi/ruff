How To Use:

1) Please press the 'Initialize Scene' button on the right to initialize all the scene objects (Don't worry if you can't see them yet)

2) Then press the 'Activate Joystick' button on the right

3) Place your mouse inside the joystick box and move your cursor within it to control the camera!

The main highlight here is the Cam-View panel which shows you the camera's point of view. The other panels are mostly for reference/learning/testing purposes. This is a a program for experimenting with the different components of the 3D engine.
